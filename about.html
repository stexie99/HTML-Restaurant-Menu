Origin Story

In 1988, Mr. ML took his wife and three kids from a rural village in Thailand to New York City. Mr.ML worked as a line cook for an Asian fusion restaurant, while his wife babysat. ML was never satisfied with the Thai food NYC has to offer, after working for 3 years him and his wife decided to start their own restaurant: H-Thai-ML, named after himself, Hector ML. Today, H-Thai-ML is a national sensation, with restaurants from coast to coast.
